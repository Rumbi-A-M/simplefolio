About me

I’m Sihle Sibiya, an Industrial Designer passionate about crafting human-centered, sustainable products that truly make a difference. From CAD modeling to prototyping, I bring innovative ideas to life, creating solutions that blend functionality, beauty, and sustainability. I thrive where creativity meets technology, designing products that solve real problems while resonating with people and the planet. My mission? To turn bold concepts into tangible designs that leave a lasting impact.

I’m an active and curious individual who loves watching YouTube, running, hitting the gym, and playing football.

Projects

Festool PowerStation

The requirements for this project are to create a rechargeable battery-powered appliance that uses the rechargeable battery of a power tool brand. I’ve chosen FESTOOL as my selected brand and the aim is to create a garden, kitchen, or home environment appliance. The design considerations maintain that the product must be innovative, practical & user centred. It should be injection moulded – functional & aesthetically pleasing, keeping in line to the corporate identity (CI) of the chosen brand.
